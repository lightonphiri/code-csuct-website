I am interested in Software Engineering; Information Retrieval, Digital Libraries, Computer Science Education, Database Systems, and Education Technology.

M.Sc. Research Overview

I am investigating principled digital library design, and more specifically exploring the feasibility and effect/impact of simple digital library architectures. I am based in the Digital Libraries Laboratory where I work under the supervision of Professor Hussein Suleman.
