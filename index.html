I am presently a doctoral student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Cape Town. I am based in the Centre for ICT for Development, where I work with A/Prof. Hussein Suleman and co-supervised by Prof. Dr. Christoph Meinel. I am exploring organised technology-driven orchestration because I would like to understand how the organisation of learning activities influences the effectiveness of educators in formal learning spaces.

I received my Bachelors degree in Computer Science from the University of Zambia in 2007. I spent the following three+ years working as a Business Analyst and Oracle DBA for Bhati Airtel---a multi-national mobile telecommunications company. In 2013, I was awarded a Masters degree in Computer Science from the University of Cape Town, having completed a dissertation titled 'Simple Digital Libraries'.

My current research interests include:

  • Computer Science Education
  • Digital Libraries
  • Technology Enhanced Learning
