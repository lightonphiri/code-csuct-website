About Me

I am a Lecturer in the Department of Library and Information Science at The University of Zambia (UNZA), where I teach Information Sciences. I conduct research in Applied Computing, with a bias towards Education---presently, my main research interests lie in computer science education, digital libraries, learning analytics, and technology-enhanced learning.

Before joining UNZA, I was a Ph.D student at the University of Cape Town (UCT).
